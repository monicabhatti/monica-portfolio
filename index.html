About Me

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, where I developed a strong foundation in AI, data analysis, and algorithm design.

I previously served as a STEM teacher, making tech education fun and accessible for young learners. Currently, I’m working as an accountant at Nimrah Public High School, where I handle student records, manage fee structures, and perform data-related tasks using Excel. I also prepare examination papers using Microsoft Office and manage the school’s social media presence to boost engagement and outreach.

On the creative side, I managed the social media for JQ Aesthetic, a skincare and wellness clinic based in Peshawar, and also worked with Live Again as a Social Media Manager and Graphic Designer, where I combined my passion for marketing, design, and creativity.

My experiences include data typing and documentation work skills that enhance my precision and attention to detail in every project.

Additionally, I have contributed as a virtual assistant for Masches Art Studio, served as the focal person and event manager for The Order of Pen, and worked as a technical writer, event manager, social media marketer, and report analyst at Triangle PR.

Alongside these roles, I have completed freelance projects focused on Power BI dashboards, Excel data analysis, KPI tracking sheets, and business reports.
