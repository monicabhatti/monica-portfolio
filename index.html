About Me

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, where I built a strong foundation in AI, data analysis, and algorithm design. My Final Year Project, "Transformer-based Unmasking Deception of DeepFake," reflected my deep interest in machine learning and digital deception.

Currently, I work as an Accountant at Nimrah Public High School, managing student records, fee data, and social media outreach while supporting administrative tasks through Excel and Microsoft Office.

I’ve also served as a STEM teacher, introducing young learners to technology in engaging ways. On the creative side, I’ve worked as a Graphic Designer and Social Media Manager for Live Again and JQ Aesthetic, combining marketing, content creation, and design to enhance brand visibility.

My experience extends to data documentation, Power BI dashboards, Excel analytics, and KPI tracking, as well as contributions as a Virtual Assistant for Masches Art Studio, and an Event Manager and Technical Writer for Triangle PR and The Order of Pen.
